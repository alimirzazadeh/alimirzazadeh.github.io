Hi. I'm Ali Mirzazadeh

I'm a Master's student at Georgia Institute of Technology, studying Machine Learning with an emphasis in healthcare. I received my Bachelor's in Computer Science and a minor in Biomedical Engineering from Georgia Tech. My studies have all been generously funded by the Stamps President's ship, Georgia Tech's full-ride program.

My goal is to use technology to solve pertinent problems in physiological monitoring and healthcare, and to pursue these solutions to real-world implementation. Recognizing that innovations in this area require advances in sensing technologies as well as applied machine learning, my experience ranges hardware, algorithm design, and machine learning.
